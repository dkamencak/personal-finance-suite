Glossary
FIRE
Financial Independence, Retire Early
The goal is to accumulate enough invested capital so that passive income (4% per year) covers all living expenses — forever. Formula: annual expenses × 25. After that, you never have to work out of necessity again.
Coast FIRE
Coasting to the finish line
You've reached the point where you no longer need to invest a single euro — your portfolio will grow to your FIRE number on its own through compound interest. You can stop aggressive saving and start spending more on yourself.
Barista FIRE
Semi-retirement freedom
Your portfolio covers most expenses, but not all. You work part-time or occasionally — just enough to cover the small "gap". You leave full-time employment and only do work you genuinely enjoy.
Projection is an approximation. Actual returns vary. Not financial advice.
